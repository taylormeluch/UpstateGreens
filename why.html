Why Buy Our Microgreens?

Support Local Businesses

There are many benefits to eating micro greens such as for their flavor, health benefits and presentation. Microgreens have the same flavor as mature plants, but the vitamins and flavor are more intense and concentrated. Using microgreens adds texture and flavor to any meal.

Microgreens have MAJOR health benefits. They are a superfood packed with nutrients and antioxidants. They provide higher nutritional value than a mature plant, up to 40 times more nutrients. Vitamin C is abundant. Vitamin E is abundant, eating Daikon radish microgreens will cover your daily requirement for this antioxidant vitamin. Vitamin K is abundant. Many microgreens are loaded with Beta-Carotene, which helps reduce the risk of disease. Many are loaded with Chlorophyll.

Most high end restaurants add microgreens for presentation value too. Microgreens can be added to many daily dishes at home as well.

Specific microgreen benefits:

Broccoli: earthly and mild in flavor: rich in Vitamin A, C, K, Folate, Calcium, Phosphorus, Potassium, and Chlorophyll. Specific health benefits- cancer prevention, detoxifies blood, helps teeth, bones, and eyes: balances pH levels.

Kale: earthy and mild in flavor: rich in Vitamin A, C, K, calcium, iron (more iron than beef), magnesium, Chlorophyll, Amino Acids. Health benefits- anti-inflammatory, antioxidants, helps eyes, skin, and bones, reduces risk of heart disease and cancer.

Radish: spicy and intense: rich in vitamin C, K, and especially E, folate, fiber, and iron. Health benefit- cancer prevention, stabilizes blood sugar control, lowers blood pressure, detoxes the body.

Mustard: spicy and colorful: rich in Vitamin A,C, K, folic acid, calcium, magnesium, and packed with phytonutrients. Health benefits- detoxes the body, may reduce cancer risk, soothes inflammation, improves eye, heart, and bone.
